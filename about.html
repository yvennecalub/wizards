Being a good

Global citizen

A good global citizen is aware of the wider world, takes an active role in their community, and works with others for a peaceful and sustainable planet. They live in a community with moral, ethical, political, and economic responsibilities. These responsibilities include

Cultural Exchange and Diversity Appreciation

  • Engage in cultural exchange programs, clubs, or events to learn about different cultures, traditions, and perspectives.
  • Respect and celebrate diversity on campus, and encourage others to do the same.

Community Service and Volunteering

  • Participate in community service projects or volunteer opportunities that address local and global issues.
  • Work with organizations that focus on humanitarian causes, such as poverty alleviation, education, or environmental conservation.

Educational Outreach

  • Share your knowledge and experiences with peers through presentations, workshops, or discussions.
  • Encourage others to become global citizens by raising awareness and fostering a sense of responsibility.

Sustainability Initiatives

  • Get involved in sustainability projects on campus, such as recycling programs, energy conservation efforts, or eco-friendly events.
  • Promote sustainable practices and encourage your school to adopt environmentally responsible policies.
